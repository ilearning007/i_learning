Structure

As we remember the Third conditional, is used to expres a situation in the past that didn't happend. We also see the structure used in every case of the Third conditional as positive, negatice, prostasis inverse, direct question and WH question.

Positive

Negative


Adopsis If Prostasis

Question

Would,+subject+have+verb past participle If Subject+past perfect sentence
